Cadmus for the study of the ancient grammatical tradition

Projects

This is the editing dashboard for a cluster of research projects related to the ancient grammatical tradition:

  • PAGES - Priscian's Ars Grammatica in European Scriptoria. A Millennium of Latin and Greek Scholarship. ERC Grant 882588, 2021-2025. PI Prof. Michela Rosellini (Roma Sapienza)
  • TAL - The Transmission of Ancient Linguistics: Texts and Contexts of the Roman Grammatical Studies / Le tradizioni della linguistica antica: testi e contesti degli studi grammaticali nel mondo romano. PRIN 2017. Coordinators: Prof. Mario De Nonno (national PI, Roma Tre unit); Prof. Claudio Giammona (Roma Sapienza unit); Prof. Rolando Ferri (Pisa unit); Prof. Paolo De Paolis (Verona unit)
  • ThDS - Thesaurus dubii sermonis: Digital Critical Collection of Ancient Latin Linguistics (1st century BC – 8th century CE). A. von Humboldt Stiftung Forschungsstipendium für erfahrene Wissenschaftler 2020. PI: Dr. Elena Spangenberg Yanes (Thesaurus linguae Latinae, Bayerische Akademie der Wissenschaften)
  • Thesaurus dubii sermonis: repertorio critico digitale della riflessione linguistica antica (I sec. a. C. – VIII sec. d. C.). Research grant of Sapienza University of Rome 2018. PI: Prof. Michela Rosellini (Sapienza University of Rome).
